Travel Nursing

Alegiant Healthcare specializes in staffing Registered Nurses (RNs), Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs), Licensed Vocational Nurses (LVNs), Certified Nurse Aids (CNAs), Surg Technologists, Phlebotomists, Dieticians, and Sterile Processing.

Alegiant’s goal is to provide top overall opportunities for our travel nurses, so that they can have the best work/ travel experience. We pride ourselves on placing our employees at some of the nation’s top healthcare facilities, where they can advance their skills and experience the freedoms and joys of traveling

Services we offer:

  • Travel/contract placements of all modalities for medical facilities
  • Contract to direct
  • Direct hire

Let us get you traveling!
